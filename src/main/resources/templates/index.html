    Traffic Offenders Project

    Welcome to the Traffic Offenders Project

    In the Spring module, we will be developing a project - Traffic Offenders.

    In the system, there are two roles: regular users and traffic inspectors.

    Users can submit descriptions of traffic violations.

    In their submission, they provide:

    • Address where the violation occurred
    • Vehicle's license plate number
    • Description of the violation
    • Photo depicting the violation

    Each submission carries a status:

    • Accepted
    • Rejected
    • Completed
